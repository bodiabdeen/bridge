Opening Leads (Against NT Contracts)

  • 4th Highest: Lead 4th highest from your longest and strongest suit.
  • Rule of 11: Subtract the card value from 11 to know how many higher cards are in other hands.
  • Top of Sequence: Lead the top of a solid sequence (e.g., QJ10).
